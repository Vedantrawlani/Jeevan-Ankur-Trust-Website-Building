About NGO

Jeevan Ankur Trust is dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities by providing education to around 150 students from economically weaker backgrounds.
Along with academic support, the trust ensures that children receive nutritious meals for their overall well-being.
Expanding its mission of care and compassion,
the organization is also working towards supporting senior citizens and currently runs an old age home in Kurla, where nearly 10 elders are provided with shelter, food, and emotional support.

Old Age Home

Jeevan Ankur Vridhashram is a compassionate initiative by Jeevan Ankur Trust dedicated to providing a safe, comfortable, and caring environment for senior citizens in Mumbai. We ensure that elders live with dignity, respect, and emotional support in a family-like atmosphere. Our mission is to offer shelter, healthcare, nutritious food, and personal care while promoting happiness and social well-being among residents. With around 10 elders currently supported, we strive to create a home where no senior feels lonely or abandoned. Through dedicated staff and a nurturing environment, we aim to bring comfort, care, and dignity to the lives of our elders.
