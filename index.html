About Jeevan Ankur Trust

Jeevan Ankur Trust works for the education of vulnerable and underprivileged communities, women empowerment, and child rights. Beneficiaries are mainly children of single parents and children from slum communities. We currently work with around 300 children and run a Vridhashram in Kurla caring for around 20 seniors.
